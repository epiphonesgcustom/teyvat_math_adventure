# Question 6: Probability (At Least One)

**Question ID:** Mixed-6  
**Source:** Exercise Mixed - Question 6 - Page 999  
**Question Number:** 6/20 (Revision 2)

### Question Text
Detective Gumshoe flips a fair coin twice. What is the probability of getting at least one head? Express as a fraction.

### Result
✅ **OBJECTION SUSTAINED (Correct)**

**My Answer:** 3/4  
**Correct Answer:** 3/4

### Solution Verification
Using complement rule:
- P(at least 1 head) = 1 - P(both tails)
- P(both tails) = 1/2 × 1/2 = 1/4
- P(at least 1 head) = 1 - 1/4 = 3/4 ✓
