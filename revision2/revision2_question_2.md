# Question 2: Cylinder Volume

**Question ID:** Mixed-2  
**Source:** Exercise Mixed - Question 2 - Page 999  
**Question Number:** 2/20 (Revision 2)

### Question Text
Maya has a cylindrical container with radius 4 cm and height 10 cm. Find the volume to 1 decimal place.

### Result
✅ **OBJECTION SUSTAINED (Correct)**

**My Answer:** 502.7  
**Correct Answer:** 502.7 cm³

### Solution Verification
Using cylinder volume formula: V = πr²h
- V = π × 4² × 10
- V = π × 16 × 10
- V = 160π ≈ 502.7 cm³ ✓
