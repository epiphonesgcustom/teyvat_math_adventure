### Question Text
Trucy needs to find the unknown leg of a right triangle. The hypotenuse is 17 cm and one leg is 15 cm. Find the other leg.

### Result

**My Answer:** 8  
**Correct Answer:** 8 cm

### Solution Verification
Using Pythagorean theorem:
- a² + b² = c²
- 15² + b² = 17²
- 225 + b² = 289
- b² = 64
- b = 8 cm ✓

Note: This is the 8-15-17 Pythagorean triple
