Question 8 · 15D Q2a
Find, to 1 decimal place, all unknown sides and angles in the triangle below.
Show answer
Right angle at top-right. Known: hypotenuse = 10 cm, one leg = 7 cm.

Find x (top side):
x² + 7² = 10² → x² = 51 → x ≈ 7.1 cm

Find θ (top-left angle):
sin θ = opp/hyp = 7/10 → θ = sin⁻¹(0.7) ≈ 44.4°

Find φ (bottom angle):
φ = 90° − 44.4° ≈ 45.6°

Part 4 — Angles of Elevation & Depression (15E)

Question 9 · 15E Q1
A wheelchair ramp is 5 m long and rises 60 cm. Find the angle θ that the ramp makes with the ground.
Show answer
Convert: 60 cm = 0.6 m. The ramp (hypotenuse) = 5 m, opposite = 0.6 m.
sin θ = 0.6 / 5 = 0.12
θ = sin⁻¹(0.12)
θ ≈ 6.9°
Question 10 · 15E Q3

tan 6° = opp/adj → adj = 25 / tan 6°
