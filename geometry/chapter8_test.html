Question 1 · 8A Q2
An isosceles triangle with base 7.8 cm has a perimeter of 30 cm. Find the length of the equal sides.

Show answer

= 2π(6) + 2π(5) = 12π + 10π = 22π ≈ 69.1 cm

Area = π(6²) − π(5²) = 36π − 25π = 11π ≈ 34.6 cm²

Part 3 — Surface Area (8C)

Question 6 · 8C Q1a
Find the surface area of the triangular prism.
Show answer
Hypotenuse = √(3² + 4²) = √25 = 5 cm
Triangle area = ½ × 3 × 4 = 6 cm²

SA = 2 × (triangle) + 3 rectangular faces
= 2(6) + (3×6) + (4×6) + (5×6)
= 12 + 18 + 24 + 30
SA = 84 cm²
Question 7 · 8C Q1d
Find the surface area of the square-based pyramid.
Show answer
SA = base area + 4 triangular faces
= 40² + 4 × (½ × 40 × 29)
= 1600 + 4 × 580
= 1600 + 2320
SA = 3920 mm²
