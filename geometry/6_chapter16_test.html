Part 2 — Sine Rule (16C)

Question 6 · 16C Q1a
Find x, rounded to 2 decimal places.
Show answer
Angles: 32° and 46°. Third angle = 180° − 32° − 46° = 102°.
Side opposite 32° = 15 cm. Side x is opposite 46°.
By sine rule: x / sin 46° = 15 / sin 32°
x = 15 × sin 46° / sin 32°
x = 15 × 0.7193 / 0.5299
x ≈ 20.36 cm

Question 8 · 16C Q3
Find the area of the triangle with angles 40° and 116° and base 6 cm.
Show answer
Third angle = 180° − 40° − 116° = 24°.
Base = 6 cm is opposite the 40° angle. Find side opposite 116°.
a / sin 116° = 6 / sin 40°
a = 6 × sin 116° / sin 40° = 6 × 0.8988 / 0.6428 ≈ 8.39 cm

Now use area formula with sides 6 cm and 8.39 cm, included angle 24°:
Area = ½ × 6 × 8.39 × sin 24°
= ½ × 50.34 × 0.4067
Area ≈ 10.2 cm²

Part 3 — Cosine Rule (16D)

Question 9 · 16D Q1a
Find the length of the remaining side.
Show answer
Two sides: 4 cm and 5 cm. Included angle: 32°.
c² = a² + b² − 2ab cos C
c² = 4² + 5² − 2(4)(5) cos 32°
c² = 16 + 25 − 40 × 0.8480
c² = 41 − 33.92 = 7.08
c = √7.08
c ≈ 2.66 cm

Question 11 · 16D Q2b
Find the value of θ, rounded to 1 decimal place.
Show answer
Sides: a = 6.7 cm, b = 4.1 cm, c = 8.3 cm. Find θ opposite to 8.3 cm.
cos θ = (a² + b² − c²) / (2ab)
cos θ = (6.7² + 4.1² − 8.3²) / (2 × 6.7 × 4.1)
cos θ = (44.89 + 16.81 − 68.89) / 54.94
cos θ = (−7.19) / 54.94 = −0.1308…
θ = cos⁻¹(−0.1308)
θ ≈ 97.5°

Part 4 — Applications (16E)

Question 12 · 16E Q5
Hazel's triangular property has sides 314 m, 407 m, and 238 m.
a) Find the angle at A (opposite the 238 m side), to 2 decimal places.
b) Find the area of the property, to the nearest hectare.
Show answer
a) Find angle A using cosine rule:
cos A = (314² + 407² − 238²) / (2 × 314 × 407)
cos A = (98 596 + 165 649 − 56 644) / 255 796
cos A = 207 601 / 255 796 = 0.8116…
A = cos⁻¹(0.8116)
A ≈ 35.72°

b) Area:
Area = ½ × 314 × 407 × sin 35.72°
= ½ × 127 798 × 0.5842
= 37 321 m²
1 hectare = 10 000 m²
Area ≈ 4 hectares
