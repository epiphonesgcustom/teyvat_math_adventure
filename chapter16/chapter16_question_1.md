### Solution Verification
I solved this independently:
- A standard deck has 52 cards
- There are 4 suits, each with 13 cards
- Number of hearts = 13
- Probability = Favorable outcomes / Total outcomes
- P(heart) = 13/52
- Simplify: 13/52 = 1/4 ✓
